About
Lili.

Senior Digital Product Designer
Deloitte Digital · Sydney, AU

Previously: UofSydney, Pisces Boutique

I'm a Senior Digital Product Designer at Deloitte Digital, where I lead human-centred design workstreams across discovery, definition, and delivery for high-impact digital products.

My work spans complex platforms for government departments, enterprise systems, and regulated environments — including Salesforce, ServiceNow, and Power BI. I collaborate across product, engineering, and delivery to translate business strategy and user needs into scalable, production-ready solutions.

I champion accessibility, usability, and design quality in highly regulated environments, and I bridge the gap between design and engineering — leveraging technical understanding to reduce rework and accelerate delivery. I also mentor junior designers and support capability uplift across teams.

Before Deloitte, I built a broad foundation through service design, web development tutoring at the University of Sydney, and independent brand and UX work — giving me a T-shaped range from systems thinking to hands-on visual craft and front-end code.
